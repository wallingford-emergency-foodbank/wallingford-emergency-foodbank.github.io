Volunteering

To enquire about volunteering, please complete this form and email it to ridgewayfood@outlook.com.

At present our volunteers pack bags of food on a Monday between 10.00am and 12pm, on a Thursday morning, between 9.30am and 1pm and on a Friday morning from 10 - 12pm. Our volunteers also receive and sort donated food on a Wednesday morning between 9.00am and 10.30am.

At present volunteer drivers deliver the food parcels to clients between approximately 12pm and 2pm on Mondays, Thursdays and Fridays. We pay mileage currently at 45p per mile. All timings vary a little from week to week, depending on demand.

All our volunteer roles require reasonable fitness as the bags are very heavy and there is a lot of lifting and handling. We have good numbers of volunteers, so you are likely to be needed about once a month for packing and twice a month for driving.
